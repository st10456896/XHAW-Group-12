Welcome to Empowering The Nation, your trusted partner in skills development for domestic workers and gardeners. Since 2018, we have been dedicated to bridging the skills gap and unlocking potential in our community.

Empowering The Nation is a socially driven initiative founded by Precious Radebe, committed to providing high-quality training programs that upskill domestic workers and gardeners. Our mission is to empower individuals with marketable skills, enhancing their employability and entrepreneurial potential. We believe in fostering a culture of growth, self-improvement, and financial stability.
